TL;DR..
¶

Components and entities needed a way to do whatever action necessary to become in sync.

More

Components and entities can be entered, altered or even removed as the app runs. But, depending on intent, these kinds of changes may cause a component to slip out of sync.

Calling Synchronize() causes any changes to be committed, so to speak.
